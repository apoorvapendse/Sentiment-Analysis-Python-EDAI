Sentiment analysis is the process of identifying and extracting emotions, opinions, and attitudes from text, image, audio, or video data. It involves using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning techniques to analyze the content and determine the sentiment of the data.

It is used by big multinational companies such as Amazon, Microsoft, Facebook, Twitter, etc.
